WEDNESDAY MEAL 3
Lean Beef Kofta, Sweet Potato Wedges and Green Beans

Kamilla:
150g lean beef mince, 5% fat
150g sweet potato wedges
80g green beans
1 tbsp very finely diced red onion
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
Black pepper
Pinch chili flakes, optional
1 tsp olive oil or low-cal spray

Christian:
180g lean beef mince, 5% fat
200g sweet potato wedges
100g green beans
1 tbsp very finely diced red onion
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
Black pepper
Pinch chili flakes, optional
1 tsp olive oil or low-cal spray

Beef kofta method:

1. Mix the beef mince with the red onion, garlic powder, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, black pepper, and optional chili flakes.
2. Shape into koftas or small patties.
3. Cook until fully cooked through.
4. Serve with the sweet potato wedges and green beans.
